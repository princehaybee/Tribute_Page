Chief M.K.O Abiola

The President Nigeria Never Had


Here's a timeline of Bashorun M.K.O Abiola's life:

  • 1937 - Moshood Kashimawa Olawale Abiola was born into a poor family in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria on August 24.
  • 1963 - He attended the University of Glasgow , Scotland, on scholarship, graduating in 1963.
  • 1965 - He then studied at the Institute of Chartered Accountants, also in Scotland, graduating in 1965.
  • 1968 - Abiola began working for ITT Nigeria in 1968.
  • 1971 - he was its chief executive and chairman, posts he held until 1988.
  • 1988 - He was voted International Businessman of the Year.
  • 1993 - Abiola ran as the presidential candidate of the Social Democratic Party in a two-party race.
  • 1994 - arrested on a charge of treason by the military regime then led by Gen. Sani Abacha.
  • 1998 - Abiola died suddenly under mysterious circumstances, arousing suspicions of foul play, although a heart attack was officially declared to be the cause of death.
  • 1998 -the business executive, philanthropist, and politician who is hailed as a figure of democratic change in Nigeria died on July 7.
